Why is this important?

"...driver error is a factor in 94% of crashes. Although it is important to remember multiple factors contribute to all crashes, the largest portion of driver error issues involve the driver failing to recognize hazards, including distraction. Many of the most promising ADAS technologies are designed to identify and react to potential hazards faster than a human driver."
- National Safety Council (NSC)

How do we reduce accidents?

Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) have the potential to greatly improve road safety. They work by alerting or assisting drivers to prevent accidents.
